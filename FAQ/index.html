What is Acme Pennant?

Acme Pennant is the novelty and giftware division of Amalgamated Felt, Inc. the number one producer of felt and felt-like materials in North America. Established in 1954 as a strategic initiative in material utilization, Acme Pennant has served the commemorative goods market for over seven decades. Our product line leverages Amalgamated Felt’s full production capacity to produce pennants for special events, institutions, and locations of interest. The division operates under Amalgamated Felt’s commitment to vertical integration and operational efficiency. All pennants are manufactured using company-standard felt substrates and comply with relevant textile safety standards.


Do you offer custom pennants?

Custom orders are evaluated on a case-by-case basis subject to minimum order quantities and material availability. Please direct inquiries to our sales department.
